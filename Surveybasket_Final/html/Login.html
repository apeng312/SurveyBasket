About Us The Survey Basket Team

Our Project

We're a team from Carnegie Mellon University's cross-platform app class, 15237. We made this website for our 2012 fall project. This website is meant to be a platform for students to post surveys and give feedbacks to each other. We hope future 237 students and students from other classes can use this website to publish their surveys and get infomation from their peers.
